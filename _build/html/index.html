欢迎我们的文档! Welcome to our documentation!¶

引力波与相对论基本天文学课题组是中国科学院上海天文台的一个主要研究引力波天文学和相对论基本天文学的课题组。您可以在我们课题组的网站(http://www.shao.ac.cn/grefa/)获得更多相关信息。本文档提供了我们课题组常用软硬件的使用说明，同时还包含了课题组成员在平时的研究过程中整理、总结的一些经验教训，以帮助新加入的成员。本文档开源，欢迎大家一起参与编辑， 项目地址为 https://github.com/kelvin1020/Documentation_GREFA

Gravitational wave and relativistic fundamental astronomy group(GREFA Group) is a group that mainly study gravitational wave astronomy and relativistic fundamental astronomy in Shanghai Astronomical Observatory, CAS. You could get more information in our group website(http://www.shao.ac.cn/grefa/).This documentation offer the guide for the hardware and software we usually use, and the experiences during the research are also included. We hope this could be helpful for the new members.This documentation is a open source project, welcome to participate in editing, the URL is https://github.com/kelvin1020/Documentation_GREFA.
